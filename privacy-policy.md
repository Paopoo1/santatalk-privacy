# Privacy Policy – Santa Talk
Last updated: 2025-11-16

This Privacy Policy explains how “Santa Talk” (“the App”, “we”, “us”) handles your data.

## 1. Summary
- We do not collect, store, or transmit your photos/videos to our servers.
- Camera/Microphone are used only to enable on‑device video recording/calls.
- Ads are delivered by Google Mobile Ads (AdMob). In‑app purchase is available to remove ads.
- We do not sell or share personal data.

## 2. Data We Handle
- On‑device only:
  - Photos/Videos you record inside the App
  - Basic runtime info needed to operate features (e.g., whether you purchased “Remove Ads”)
- Not collected by us:
  - We do not create server accounts or upload your media to our servers.

## 3. Device Permissions and Purpose
- CAMERA: for video recording and camera preview.
- MICROPHONE (RECORD_AUDIO): for audio recording during video capture/calls.
- INTERNET/NETWORK: to load ads and perform network operations required by the OS/SDKs.
- READ/WRITE STORAGE or Media Store (if applicable): to save the video file locally on your device.

## 4. Advertising (AdMob)
We use Google Mobile Ads (AdMob) to show ads. AdMob may collect device identifiers (e.g., Advertising ID), IP address, coarse location, and app interaction signals for ad delivery, frequency capping, and measurement, in accordance with Google policies.
- Learn more about how Google uses information for ads: https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads  
- About AdMob & privacy: https://support.google.com/admob/answer/6128543

### Consent & Choices
- Where required (e.g., EEA/UK), we may present a consent dialog (Google UMP) for ad personalization and measurement. You can change your consent at any time if the dialog is offered again or via OS/Google settings.
- You can reset or limit the use of your Advertising ID in Android system settings (Ads settings) and manage ad personalization in Google’s Ads Settings: https://adssettings.google.com/

## 5. In‑App Purchases
- “Remove Ads” (non‑consumable) is provided via Google Play Billing. Google processes payment information; we do not receive your full payment details.

## 6. Children’s Privacy
- The App is intended for general audiences and is not directed at children under the age required by local laws. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

## 7. Data Sharing & Sale
- We do not sell your personal data.
- We do not share your media files with third parties. Ad delivery is handled by AdMob as described above.

## 8. Data Retention
- Media you create in the App remains on your device unless you choose to share or delete it.
- Purchase status (e.g., “Remove Ads”) is kept only as needed to provide the feature and can be restored via Google Play.

## 9. Security
- We rely on the device’s operating system security, Google Play Billing, and Google Mobile Ads SDKs. While no method is 100% secure, we apply reasonable measures to protect data processed by the App.

## 10. Your Choices
- You can deny CAMERA/MIC permissions in OS settings; some features may not work without them.
- You can purchase “Remove Ads” to disable ads.
- You can manage Advertising ID and ad personalization in Google/Android settings.

## 11. International Users
- The App does not transfer your media to our servers. Ad services may process data in various regions subject to Google’s policies.

## 12. Changes to this Policy
- We may update this policy from time to time. Continued use of the App after changes means you accept the updated policy. The “Last updated” date will be revised accordingly.
